About for this project

R3LAMP is one of the priority projects under the Central Luzon Regional Higher Education Action Plan (CLRHEAP) of CHEDRO-III that aims to: Effectively implement flexible learning, Encourage the maximum use of technnology in teaching and learning, and assist in LMS provision and training.
